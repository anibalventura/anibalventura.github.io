Basic Calculator

Cross platform iOS and Android app

Description

Chat room messaging app. Build with UIKit, MVC and Firebase as backend.

Features:

  • Basic math operations.
  • Copy the result or formula to clipboard.
  • History of past calculations.
  • Tools for other mathematical operations.
  • Ad support.
  • Splash screen.
  • Light/Dark mode.
  • Localization (English/Spanish).
