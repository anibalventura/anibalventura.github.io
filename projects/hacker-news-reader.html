Hacker News Reader

Native iOS app.

Description

News reader app, the news are fetched from Hacker News Algolia API. Build with SwiftUI and MVVM pattern.

Features:

  • List with news order by date.
  • Search bar for news order by higher points.
  • Pull to refresh.
  • Splash screen.
  • Light/Dark mode.
  • Localization (English/Spanish).
